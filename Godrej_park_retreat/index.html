Overview GODREJ PARK RETREAT PHASE 3
Premium 2/3/3.5/4.5 BHK Homes at Sarjapur Road

Welcome to GRANDEUR at Park Retreat, the ultimate luxury living destination in Sarjapur, Bangalore. Nestled in the vibrant heart of Sarjapur, GRANDEUR offers a lifestyle beyond your expectations. Boasting a 2600 m² (~28,000 sq. ft) clubhouse and over 50 lifestyle amenities, this residential enclave indulges your every desire. Experience thrilling outdoor activities and serene green spaces spread across 1.64 hectares (4.06 acres). With exceptional connectivity to major IT hubs like Whitefield, Electronic City, and Marathahalli, GRANDEUR ensures unparalleled convenience. Don’t wait for life to happen; make life happen at Godrej Park Retreat , where luxury and convenience meet.

Imagine a world where vibrance and excitement seamlessly blend with tranquility and sophistication. GRANDEUR at Park Retreat is designed to welcome you and your loved ones with open arms, leaving a lasting impression at every turn. Whether you’re passionate about sports or seeking relaxation, our extensive range of activities caters to all preferences, set against breathtaking views and world-class amenities. Located at the prime Sarjapur Road-ORR Junction, Harlur, Godrej Park Retreat offers unmatched connectivity and convenience, spread across 13 acres of lush green spaces and luxurious facilities. Choose from 1 BHK to 3 BHK luxury apartments, each designed to provide a serene view right from your doorstep. Embrace a vibrant lifestyle amidst nature at Godrej Park Retreat , where your best life awaits.
